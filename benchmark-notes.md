# Benchmark Notes

Benchmarked on April 2, 2026 while shaping the TEK Investments website.

## Similar Sites Reviewed

1. Pentiq
   Link: https://pentiq.com/
   Supporting page: https://pentiq.com/services/
   What stood out:
   - Clear hero headline with immediate contact action
   - Service grid broken into practical, easy-to-scan categories
   - Simple methodology section that explains how engagements run

2. Risk Crew
   Link: https://riskcrew.com/
   What stood out:
   - Risk-led messaging instead of overly technical copy
   - Strong differentiator section that explains why to trust the firm
   - Repeated contact calls to action that keep conversion simple

## How Those Benchmarks Influenced This Build

- Added a direct hero section with strong call-to-action buttons.
- Structured services into clean cards so TEK's offering is easier to scan.
- Highlighted TEK's methodology as a dedicated section for clarity.
- Added a focused "Why organizations choose TEK" panel to reinforce trust.
- Kept the tone practical and outcome-led, matching consultancy buyer expectations.

## Brand Direction Applied

- Primary palette follows the logo and attachment theme:
  - Purple: `#6f30a5`
  - Coral: `#fb5e59`
  - Deep plum for contrast: `#2a1638`
- Used warm neutrals and soft lavender backgrounds to keep the site polished without drifting away from the client's identity.
